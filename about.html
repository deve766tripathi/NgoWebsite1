Welcome to Our NGO

BEST(Board of Environmental and Sustainable Trust) is a non-governmental organization (NGO) dedicated to safeguarding the environment and promoting sustainable practices. Founded in 2005, BEST has grown into a global force for positive change in the face of environmental challenges. BEST mission is to protect and preserve the Earth's natural resources, biodiversity, and ecosystems. We strive to empower individuals, communities, and governments to take action for a sustainable future. Our vision is a world where people live in harmony with nature, respecting and protecting the delicate balance of the environment.

Our Mission

1.Conservation of Biodiversity: GEG works to protect endangered species, preserve habitats, and promote conservation efforts to maintain the Earth's rich biodiversity.
2.Climate Change Mitigation: Recognizing the urgent need to address climate change, GEG advocates for clean energy solutions, encourages carbon footprint reduction, and supports policies that promote renewable energy adoption.
3..Sustainable Agriculture and Forest Management: The organization focuses on promoting sustainable farming practices and responsible forest management to conserve natural resources and ensure food security.
4.Environmental Education: GEG believes that knowledge empowers change. They conduct awareness campaigns, workshops, and educational programs to engage the public, especially youth, on environmental issues.
5.Advocacy and Policy Reform: GEG actively engages with policymakers and governments at the local, national, and international levels to advocate for environmentally friendly policies and legislation.
6.Waste Management and Recycling: GEG promotes waste reduction, recycling initiatives, and responsible waste management to combat the global waste crisis.
